Chapli Masala

  • Time: 7 min _
  • Serves: 6_
Ingredients Quantity
Salt1tbsp
Ginger powder 1 tbsp
Garlic powder 1 tbsp
Red chillies flake1 tbsp
Crushed Cumin seeds1 1 tbsp
Crushed coriander seeds1 tbsp
Pomegranate seed 1 tbsp
Chaat Masala 1 tsp
Black pepper 1/2 tsp
Black salt 1/4 tsp
Garam Masala 1/4 tsp
Red chillies powder 1 tbsp

Steps :

  • Use grinder and grind or crush all whole spices.
  • mix them together
  • Voila the masala is ready to incorporate in next recipe.

Chapli Kebabs

  • Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: 6 peoples+/-
Ingredients Quantity
Mince meat1kg
ginger/garlic paste4table spoon
Chopped onions 2
chapli masla8-9 TSpn
Egg1

Steps :

  • Mix minced meat with all the ingredients
  • add egg and 1 TSp Garam flour
  • Put1 cup oil in frying pan and let it hot.
  • Make balls of mixed minced meat and pressed it so it looks like pressed fillet.
  • Fry on medium to high heat from both sides.
